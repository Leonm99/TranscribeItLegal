This Privacy Notice for Leon Mischlich (doing business as Transcribe • IT) describes how and why we might access, collect, store, use, and/or share your personal information when you use our services.

1. What Information Do We Collect?

  • Account Data: Email addresses (via Firebase/Social Login) to manage your Pro subscription status.
  • Audio & Video Data: Files you voluntarily upload for transcription.
  • Device Data: IP address, device ID, operating system (collected automatically for security).
  • Usage & Analytics Data (Anonymous): We collect anonymous metrics to improve the app stability and user experience.
Detailed Analytics (Firebase)

We log specific events to understand how the app is used. No personal identifiers (like email or name) are linked to these events. You can disable this collection at any time in the App Settings > Usage Analysis.

  • General Interaction: App first open, session start, and screen views.
  • Feature Usage: Statistics on transcription starts (e.g., file count) and success (e.g., text length), as well as usage of AI features (Summarization, Translation).
  • Subscription Events: Interactions with the billing screen (e.g., clicking a price, subscription updates/cancellations via Google Play).
  • Performance & Stability: Startup time, API response times (HTTP status), and crash reports.

2. How Do We Process Your Information?

We process your information to provide services (transcription via Groq/OpenAI), manage accounts, process payments, and improve app performance through anonymous analytics.

3. Legal Bases (GDPR)

We rely on the following legal bases to process your personal information:

  • Performance of a Contract: To deliver the transcription and AI services you requested.
  • Consent: For optional analytics (which you can revoke via Settings).
  • Legitimate Interests: To diagnose technical issues, prevent fraud, and improve the application's functionality based on anonymous usage patterns.

4. Sharing Your Information

We share data strictly with the following third-party vendors under Data Processing Addendums (DPAs):

  • Groq, Inc.: Speech-to-Text processing.
  • OpenAI, LLC: Translation and summarization.
  • Google (Firebase & Analytics): Authentication, Database, and anonymous usage analysis.
AI & Data Training: Your uploaded content is NOT used to train the AI models of our providers (Groq/OpenAI).

5. Data Retention

Uploaded audio/video files are deleted from active processing buffers shortly after transcription ("Ephemeral Processing"). Text transcripts are stored locally or in the database until you delete them.

6. Your Privacy Rights

In the EEA/UK, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict processing, and withdraw consent.

Right to Object (Analytics): You may opt-out of anonymous analytics tracking directly within the app under "Settings".

For other requests, contact us at app.transcribe.it@gmail.com.
